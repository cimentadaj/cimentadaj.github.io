About


Hi, I’m Jorge Cimentada and I’m a PhD candidate in Sociology at Pompeu Fabra University in Barcelona, Spain. I belong to the Research and Expertise Centre for Survey Methodology (RECSM) and my main research interests are the study of achievement inequality, the role of schools in reproducing these inequalities and early education as a remedy to achievement inequalities. I’m currently working at the Research and Expertise Centre for Survey Methodology (RECSM) as an R developer and Data Scientists. Previously I worked on a project related to class mobility and how it has changed over time in industrialized countries in the DEMOSOC research group.

I am very passionate about statistical programming with R , data visualization, statistics and drums. In fact, this blog is kindly linked to the amazing R-bloggers website. You should check it out!

If you wish to contact me send me an email to cimentadaj@gmail.com. I’m also on Github and Linkedin.
