About

Hi! I’m Jorge Cimentada, Senior Data Scientist at eDreams Odigeo and a Professor at University Charles III of Madrid. I’ve worked in data science ever since I earned a PhD in Social Sciences in 2019 where I used computational methods to understand inequality in educational systems. I also like to build open source tools for data wrangling, data science and more broadly machine learning. If you’d like to learn more about what I do, have a look at some of my projects or courses:

  • Open source software related to statistical estimations, API wrappers, data acquisition.
  • I’ve taught many courses over the years, two of them are on Machine Learning and Data Harvesting.
  • I regularly do consulting work related to machine learning and statistical software development. Some of my work includes the United Nations Population Division and the Max Planck Institute for Demographic Research.
  • For CV related things check out my linkedin profile.

Outside of my work life, I have a band called YEITUEI where I play drums.
